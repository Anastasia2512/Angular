The staff, students, parents and community of Cool High School partner to foster successful learning through effective teaching. The high school's mission is to challenge all students to reach their potential and to develop responsible citizens in a caring and supportive environment. Cool High School offers a comprehensive college-prep curriculum for students in grades 9-12. Students can choose from approximately 190 educational options including 27 Advanced Placement and Honors courses. Students also can choose Vocational Education and Career Education Programs at the Oakland Technical Center. Additionally, extra-curricular options range from Forensics, Guitar Club and Equestrian Team to Quiz Bowl, Rotary Interact and National Honor Society.
